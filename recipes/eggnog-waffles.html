Eggnog Waffles

Description

"Eggnog Waffles" are a breakfast dish made by incorporating eggnog into the waffle batter. These waffles have a festive and rich flavor, with the characteristic taste of eggnog, which often includes ingredients like eggs, milk, sugar, and spices such as nutmeg or cinnamon. Eggnog waffles are a delightful seasonal variation on traditional waffles and can be enjoyed with a variety of toppings like maple syrup, whipped cream, or fruit for a delicious and comforting breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups eggnog
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl. Add eggnog, eggs, and butter; mix well.
  2. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions. Pour 1/4 of the batter into the preheated iron and cook until waffle is crisp and golden, about 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
